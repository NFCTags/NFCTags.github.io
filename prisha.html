About Me

Hi! My name's Prisha, and I'm a design engineer for Team Apollo. I've always had interest in engineering and F1 in Schools was a great opportunity to explore my interest. As part of my role as front wing engineer, I learned about the principles of aerodynamics and did extensive research on F1 cars previously in the competition and racing on the track today. Gathering this knowledge, I used my CAD skills to design the front wing which was part of the car we raced in the competition!
